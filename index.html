About Global Kids

A Place Where Every Child Truly Belongs

Founded in 2009, Global Kids International School has been a beacon of academic excellence and holistic development in Bengaluru. We believe every child is gifted and our mission is to unlock their fullest potential.

Our world-class faculty, state-of-the-art infrastructure, and progressive pedagogy create an environment where learning is a joy, not a burden.

Our Campus

World-Class Facilities

Our campus is built to inspire — every space is designed to spark creativity, collaboration, and excellence.

Smart Classrooms

Interactive digital boards, high-speed internet, and AI-powered learning tools in every classroom.

Sports Complex

Football ground, basketball courts, cricket pitch, swimming pool, and indoor games hall.

Performing Arts Centre

A 500-seat auditorium with professional lighting, acoustics, and dedicated dance and music studios.

Science & STEM Labs

State-of-the-art physics, chemistry, biology labs alongside a dedicated robotics and maker space.

Library & Resource Centre

Over 15,000 books, digital archives, and a calm reading environment for every student.

Safe Transport

GPS-tracked AC buses with lady attendants covering 40+ routes across Bengaluru.
